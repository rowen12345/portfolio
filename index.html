Research Intern · 2024-2025
AI models to predict IV curves of perovskite solar cells

Conducted research under Erik Birgersson at the National University of Singapore, developing AI models in MATLAB to predict current-voltage (I-V) curves of perovskite solar cells using 31 input parameters, reducing required prediction points from 45 to 10 while maintaining accuracy; the full curve was reconstructed using Piecewise Cubic Hermite Interpolation (PCHIP) and spline interpolation, achieving a mean RMSE of 5 across more than 6,600 predictions, with neural networks optimized through extensive hyperparameter tuning and an interactive MATLAB App Designer tool built for real-time I-V curve prediction based on user-input parameters.
